### Issue: Set the License File Correctly

#### Importance of Proper Licensing
Setting the correct license for your project is crucial as it defines how others can use, modify, and distribute your code. A proper license helps to protect your rights as a creator while also providing clarity to others who may want to utilize your work. It can enhance collaboration and foster community contributions.

#### Suggested License Options:
1. **MIT License**  
   - A permissive license that is simple and easy to understand. It allows for reuse within proprietary software, making it a popular choice.

2. **Apache License 2.0**  
   - Provides an express grant of rights to use, reproduce, and distribute the software under specific terms. It also includes an explicit grant of patent rights from contributors.

3. **GNU General Public License (GPL)**  
   - A copyleft license that ensures that any modified versions of the project are also open source. Ideal for projects that want to ensure future contributions remain free.

4. **BSD License**  
   - Similar to the MIT License but with additional clauses related to the use of the project's name and derived products.
